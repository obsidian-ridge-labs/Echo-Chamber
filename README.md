## What is Echo Chamber

Echo Chamber is a private transcription app for people who cannot send their conversations to a server. Lawyers, doctors, journalists, and executives use it to capture meetings, interviews, and lectures with absolute confidentiality. It transcribes in real time, identifies who said what, summarizes in the format you need, and lets you ask your transcript questions, all without an internet connection.

## How it works

1. **Record or import.** Tap to record live audio, or import an existing audio or video file. It works in airplane mode.
2. **Transcribe on-device.** The Parakeet TDT v3 neural engine turns speech into text on your phone's Neural Engine, in real time.
3. **Summarize with AI.** Generate Cornell Notes, Meeting Minutes, Bullet Summary, Outline, Q&A, or an Executive Summary, all processed on-device.
4. **Export or ask AI.** Export to PDF, DOCX, SRT, VTT, Markdown, JSON, or text. Or ask AI questions about what was said.

## Features

- **On-device transcription** powered by Parakeet TDT v3, with a WhisperKit fallback on lighter hardware
- **25 European languages** with automatic detection
- **Speaker identification** with voice profiles that learn over time
- **6 AI summary formats**: Cornell Notes, Outline, Meeting Minutes, Bullet Summary, Q&A Interview, Executive Summary
- **AI Chat** over your transcript, on-device
- **8 export formats**: plain text, timestamped text, SRT, VTT, Markdown, JSON, PDF, DOCX
- **Audio and video import** for transcribing existing files
- **Face ID lock**, full-text search, Spotlight, widgets, and Live Activities
- **Optional encrypted iCloud sync**, off by default, with audio encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM

## Privacy

No audio is ever uploaded. Echo Chamber has no servers, no accounts, and no data collection. Every feature works offline, including transcription, summaries, speaker identification, and search. If you turn on iCloud sync, your recordings travel through your own personal iCloud container, which is Apple's infrastructure rather than ours, encrypted with a key in your iCloud Keychain. Read the full policy at https://obsidianridgelabs.com/privacy.
