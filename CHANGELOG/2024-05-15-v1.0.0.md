# Echo Chamber v1.0.0

**Released:** 2024-05-15
**Platform:** iOS 18+, with macOS and watchOS companions

The first public release of Echo Chamber. Private, 100% offline AI transcription.

## Highlights

- On-device transcription powered by Parakeet TDT v3, with a WhisperKit fallback
- Real-time speech to text in 25 European languages with automatic detection
- Speaker identification with voice profiles that learn over time
- AI summaries in 6 formats: Cornell Notes, Outline, Meeting Minutes, Bullet Summary, Q&A Interview, Executive Summary
- AI Chat over your transcript, on-device
- Export to 8 formats: plain text, timestamped text, SRT, VTT, Markdown, JSON, PDF, DOCX
- Audio and video import, Face ID lock, full-text search, Spotlight, widgets, and Live Activities
- Optional encrypted iCloud sync, off by default

For setup help and feature guides, visit https://obsidianridgelabs.com/help/echochamber.
